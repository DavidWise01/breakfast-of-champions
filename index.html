The People
Dwayne Hoover
Prosperous but cracking-up Pontiac dealer whose madness erupts into violence.
Kilgore Trout
The neglected SF writer summoned to an arts festival — his book triggers Dwayne's breakdown.
Rabo Karabekian
Abstract-expressionist painter whose work delivers the redemptive 'band of light' epiphany.
Wayne Hoobler
Black parolee drifting around Dwayne's dealership — the foil exposing American cruelty.
Kurt Vonnegut (as author)
The narrator intrudes into his own story, meets Trout, and finally frees him.
